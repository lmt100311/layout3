About Pollution




What is pollution?

Poluution is a worldwide problem and it is happened by throwing trash, plastic bottles and many more. It is very bad for our planet Earth.

Different types of pollution

There are many types of pollution on Earth. They are very bad and some examples are

  • Water Pollution
  • Land Pollution

    • How can we reduce pollution?

    There are many differents ways of reducing pollutions. We can recycle many things such as plastic, furniture and many more. We can also reduce waste such as plastic bottles, plastic bags and many more things. You can also reuse plastic bottles and things that are not too old. There are also ways to create something into a great product. We can use plastic bottles to create pots to put flowers and plants. It takes some time but it is worth it and it is also a way of helping the environment. There is another way called upcycling. It is a little similar with recycling but upcycling only use waste and turn something into a new one which is more valuable than the old ones. It is sometimes called as "creative reuse".
